# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project respectful, welcoming,
and focused on learning and building useful security software.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Discuss technical disagreements with evidence.
- Avoid harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Do not share private data, secrets, or unauthorized exploit details.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Encouraging unauthorized access or harmful activity.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict
participation when behavior harms the project or community.
